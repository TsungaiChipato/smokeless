Quick solutions!

Take a break!

Sometimes you may not have a lot time during a break and its easy to look for a quick fix. Trying one of these short craving busters can help!

Drink a glass of water

With dehydration, the skin can become more vulnerable to skin disorders and premature wrinkling.

Do three sets of ten jumping jacks.

Jumping jack is an aerobic cardio exercise, meaning, you use oxygen to meet the energy demands and stimulate the heart muscles.

Suck on a piece of candy.

Chewing candies lowers your stress levels, improves your mood and helpful in mental health as well.

Floss and brush your teeth.

In fact, there's an increasing amount of evidence linking periodontal disease to an increased risk of heart disease.
